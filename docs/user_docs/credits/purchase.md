# Purchase Credits

Buy credit packs to fund activities on POPskills. Payments are processed securely
through our payment provider.

---

## Browse credit packs

Navigate to **Dashboard > Finance & Analytics > Credits** and click **Purchase credits**,
or go directly to **Dashboard > Finance & Analytics > Credit Packs**.

Available packs are displayed sorted by credit amount (ascending). Each pack shows:

- **Pack name**
- **Number of credits**
- **Price**

---

## Complete a purchase

1. Select the credit pack you want to buy.
2. Click **Purchase**.
3. You are redirected to a **secure checkout page**.
4. Enter your payment details and confirm.

---

## After payment

### Successful payment

You are redirected to a confirmation page showing:

- Transaction reference number
- Credits added to your account
- Amount paid
- New credit balance

A confetti animation celebrates the purchase.

Credits are available **immediately** after a successful payment and are valid for **1 year** from the purchase date. An invoice is sent to your email. For organizations, all users in the organization can use the newly purchased credits.

### Failed or cancelled payment

If the payment fails or you cancel:

- No credits are added to your account.
- No amount is charged.
- You are redirected to an error page with troubleshooting tips:
  - Check that your card has sufficient funds
  - Verify your card information
  - Contact your bank if the issue persists
- Click **Retry purchase** to try again.
