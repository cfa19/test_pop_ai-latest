# Browse Activities

The activity catalog lets you discover and filter all available activities on the platform.

---

## Access the catalog

Navigate to **Dashboard > Activities** from the sidebar menu.

The list displays activities sorted by creation date (newest first).

---

## What you see

Each activity card shows:

- Activity **title** and **description**
- **Activity type**
- **Category** label
- **Credit cost**
- **Status** (active, draft, archived)

---

## Filter by category

Use the category filter at the top of the list to narrow results. Categories are defined by
administrators and may include labels such as *Evaluation*, *Accompaniment*, *Validation*, or
*Marketing*.

---

## Role-based visibility

The activities you see depend on your role:

| Role | Visible activities |
|---|---|
| **Beneficiary** | Only activities assigned to you |
| **Consultant** / **Org Admin** | Activities belonging to your organization |
| **Individual User** | All active public activities |
