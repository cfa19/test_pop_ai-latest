# Runners

Every activity on POPskills is powered by a runner — the engine that defines the interactive experience. This document describes every runner available on the platform: its function and what the user does.

---

## Onboarding

### Onboarding Talent Market Fit

**Function:** Provide a personalized welcome experience with baseline objectives and confidence tracking.

Complete a personalized welcome experience: set 3 personal objectives and record a baseline confidence level. Produces a personalized starting point with your objectives and initial confidence level to track progress throughout your journey.

### Vision de Reussite

**Function:** Help users define a clear 6-month professional vision with actionable commitments.

Create 3 concrete success images, set expected results, define SMART progression signals, identify obstacles, and commit to a first concrete step. Produces a structured 6-month vision plan with coaching feedback, clarity score, and commitment level.

---

### 3 Reussites Rapides

**Function:** Extract actionable strengths from recent micro-successes using structured storytelling.

### Bilan 360 Complet

**Function:** Provide a comprehensive 360-degree professional assessment with gap analysis and action plan.

Upload your CV and answer 17+ questionnaire items for a complete professional evaluation. Estimated duration: approximately 60 minutes. Produces a 360 score (0-100), gap analysis by priority, up to 5 training recommendations, and a 6-month action plan.

---

### Mini-Bilan Express

**Function:** Quickly assess professional positioning and market awareness with minimal input.

Answer 12 quick questions for a rapid positioning assessment. Produces a positioning score and market awareness score, combined into a global score, with suggestions on which modules to pursue next.

---

### Validation de l'Adequation

**Function:** Evaluate fit for a specific job opportunity across multiple dimensions.

Enter details about a specific job opportunity to evaluate your fit across 5 axes: values alignment, skills fit, timing, practical constraints, and career aspirations. Produces a GO / REVIEW / NOGO decision with green/yellow/red flags, a decision matrix, interview preparation tips, and negotiation angles.

### Test de Positionnement Initial

**Function:** Establish a baseline profile of commercial communication competencies.

Complete a 10-minute assessment of your commercial communication competencies. Produces a baseline profile with global score and per-competence breakdown, personalized recommendations, and a suggested formula (Discovery, Intermediate, or Advanced).

### Moteur Pro - Motivations et Valeurs
