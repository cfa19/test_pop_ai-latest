# Validation Workflow

The validation workflow is the end-to-end process through which activity results are reviewed,
enriched, and released to beneficiaries.

---

## The complete flow

```
Beneficiary completes activity
        |
        v
Result appears in "To Review" queue
        |
        v
Consultant reviews result detail
        |
        v
Consultant enriches (notes, recommendations, narrative, next steps)
        |
        v
Consultant releases or schedules release
        |
        v
Beneficiary sees results in "My Results"
```

---

## Validation queue

Navigate to **Dashboard > Management > Validation**.

This page is available to **Consultant** role only.

The queue has three tabs:

### To review

Completed activities waiting for consultant validation. Each item shows:

- Beneficiary name
- Activity title
- Score
- Completion date

Click an item to open the full result detail.

### In progress

Activities currently being worked on by beneficiaries. This gives consultants visibility
into active work before it reaches the review queue.

### History

Previously validated items. Provides an audit trail of past validation decisions.

---

## Statistics

Two summary cards appear at the top:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Activities in progress** | Number of activities with progress < 100% |
| **Programs in progress** | Number of programs not yet fully completed |

---

## Validation actions

From the result detail page, consultants can:

1. **Review** the activity results (summary, details, files).
2. **Enrich** with confidential notes, recommendations, and motivational narrative.
3. **Set intervention flags** for areas of concern.
4. **Release** results to the beneficiary immediately or on a scheduled date.

For program-level validation, a dedicated dialog lets you validate the entire program once
all activities are completed.

Use the "Beneficiary Preview" tab to see exactly what the beneficiary will experience before you release the results.

---

## Beneficiary notifications

When results are released, the beneficiary can see them in their
My Results page. If results are still pending, a notification
informs them that their consultant is reviewing their work.
