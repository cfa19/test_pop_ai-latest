# Beneficiary Results

Beneficiaries see their completed activity results in a gamified, encouraging format designed
to motivate continued development.

---

## Access your results

Navigate to **Dashboard > Main > My Results**.

Only results with a **released** status are visible. If your consultant has not yet reviewed and released your results, you will see a notification: *"X result(s) are being reviewed by your consultant."*

---

## Results overview

### Header

A gradient banner displays the title *"My Results & Achievements"* along with your total
badge count.

### Statistics cards

Four cards summarize your performance:

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **Available results** | Number of released results |
| **Average progress** | Overall progress percentage with a visual progress bar |
| **Actions to complete** | Number of recommended next steps across all results |
| **Recent results** | Results from the current week |

---

## Browse results

Results are displayed as cards, filterable by category:

- **Evaluations** -- Assessments and self-evaluation results
- **Accompaniment** -- Coaching and support activity results
- **Validations** -- Certification and skill validation results

Each result card shows:

- Activity name and type
- Score and completion date
- Progress indicator
- Motivational narrative from your consultant
- Recommended next steps
- Earned badges
- A "Recent" flag if completed within the last 7 days

---

## Result detail

Click a result card to see the full detail, including:

- **Hero section** with trophy icon and score
- **Progress overview** with a visual progress bar
- **Motivational narrative** -- A personalized story written by your consultant
- **Next steps** -- Actionable recommendations with difficulty levels and estimated times
- **Consultant recommendations** -- Specific guidance from your consultant
- **Gamification panel** -- Badges earned, current level, and achievements

---

## Summary

At the bottom of the results page, a summary shows:

> *"You have completed X evaluations and earned Y badges. Your average progress is Z%."*
