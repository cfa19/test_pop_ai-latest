# Settings

The settings page lets all users manage their account preferences.

---

## Access settings

Navigate to **Dashboard > Settings** from the sidebar menu.

Settings are available to all roles.

---

## Account preferences

The settings page provides options to:

- Update your **personal information** (name, email)
- Change your **password**
- Configure **notification preferences**
- Manage your **display settings**

Changes to your email or password may require re-authentication.
