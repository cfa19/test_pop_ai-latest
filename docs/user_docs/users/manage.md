# Manage Users

Organization Admins can view and manage user accounts from the user management page.

---

## Access user management

Navigate to **Dashboard > Administration > Users**.

Consultants are redirected to the **Beneficiaries** page instead, since they manage users through the beneficiary management interface.

---

## What you see depends on your role

| Role | Visible users |
|---|---|
| **Org Admin** | Only users within your organization |

---

## User list

The user list displays:

- **Name** and **email**
- **Role** (Org Admin, Consultant, Beneficiary, Individual User)
- **Organization** (if applicable)
- **Status** (active, inactive)
- **Registration date**

---

## User actions

Depending on your role and the user's status, available actions may include:

- **View profile** -- See full user details
- **Edit role** -- Change the user's role within the organization
- **Deactivate** -- Disable the user's access without deleting their data
- **Reactivate** -- Restore a deactivated user's access

Deactivating a user does not delete their data or completed activities. It only prevents them from logging in.
