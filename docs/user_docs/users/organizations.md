# Organizations

Organizations are independent entities in POPskills. Each organization has its own users,
activities, programs, credits, and data — fully isolated from other organizations.

---

## Access organization management

Navigate to **Dashboard > Administration > Organizations**.

---

## What you see depends on your role

| Role | Visible organizations |
|---|---|
| **Org Admin** | Your own organization |

---

## Organization details

Each organization record includes:

- **Name**
- **Categories** assigned to the organization
- **User count** and role breakdown
- **Credit balance**
- **Creation date**

---

## Data isolation

POPskills enforces strict data isolation between organizations:

- Users in one organization **cannot** see data from another organization.
- Credits purchased by an organization are **shared** among all users in that organization.
- Data isolation is enforced automatically at every level of the platform.

Data isolation is enforced automatically. There is no manual configuration needed.
