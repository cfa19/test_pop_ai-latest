# Create an Account

To start using POPskills, create a free account from the registration page.

---

## Choose your account type

On the registration page, select the account type that best fits your needs:

| Account type | Who is it for | Key benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Individual** | Personal and professional self-development | Access self-assessment and development activities |
| **Consultant** | Professionals who manage clients | Full assignment and results management |
| **Organization** | Teams and their beneficiaries | Centralized management, shared credits, analytics |

If you select **Organization**, an additional field appears to enter your organization name.

---

## Fill in your details

1. Enter your **First name** and **Last name**.
2. Enter your **Email** address.
3. Choose a **Password** (minimum 8 characters, must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one digit).
4. Confirm your password in the **Confirm password** field.
5. Click **Create my account**.

Use the eye icon next to the password fields to toggle password visibility.

---

## Verify your email

After registration, you are redirected to a verification page. An email is sent to the address
you provided.

1. Open your inbox and look for the verification email.
2. Click the verification link in the email.
3. You can now log in with your credentials.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or wait a few minutes.

---

## Additional roles

The **Beneficiary** role is not available during self-registration. Beneficiary accounts are
created by a consultant or organization admin when assigning users to activities.
