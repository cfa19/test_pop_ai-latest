# Log In

Access your POPskills account from the login page.

---

## Sign in

1. Navigate to the POPskills login page.
2. Enter your **Email** address.
3. Enter your **Password**.
4. Click **Sign in**.

After successful authentication, you are redirected to your **Dashboard**.

If you are already logged in and visit the login page, you are automatically redirected to the dashboard.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| "Invalid credentials" error | Double-check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. |
| Forgot your password | Click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form. |
| No account yet | Click **Don't have an account? Create a free account** to register. |
